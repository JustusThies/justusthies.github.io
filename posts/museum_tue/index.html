Cyber and the City

2023, May 12    
Justus Thies 
MUSEUM

In the exhibition, titled ‚Cyber and the City‘, under the guidance of our Cluster speaker Ulrike Luxburg and Thomas Thiemeyer, Professor for Historical and Cultural Anthropology, we presented recent works in the domain of synthetic media. Specifically, we contributed an installation where people could try facial reenactment and learn about how to spot deep fakes.

The exhibition was awarded with the DFG Communicator Award 2024: DFG Award
